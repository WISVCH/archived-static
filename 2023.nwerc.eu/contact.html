The organisation can be contacted at contact@2023.nwerc.eu. Companies interested in supporting the contest can contact extern@2023.nwerc.eu.

Organisation

The NWERC 2023 is hosted by the Delft University of Technology and organised by W.I.S.V. ‘Christiaan Huygens’.
