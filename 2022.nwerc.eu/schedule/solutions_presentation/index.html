Solutions and award ceremony

From 15:30 until 17:00 on Sunday in Aula - Auditorium

Here the jury will present the solutions to the problems. After this, the frozen part of the scoreboard is slowly revealed and the winners are announced!

Location

The solutions presentation and award ceremony take place in the Auditorium of the Aula Conference Centre. The entrance of the Auditorium is on the top floor, and indicated on the signs.

For directions, see the Location page.
