What is the Business Tour?

Find The Perfect Job, Internship & Thesis Opportunities During A Week-long Trip Filled With Company Visits In April 2024!

From April 20th to 26th, 2024, approximately 30 late-bachelor's and master's students from both Applied Mathematics and Computer Science will embark on a week-long company orientation tour. The purpose is to provide an inspiring introduction to interesting companies, allowing students to branch out into all kinds of different career paths. The Business Tour presents a unique opportunity to connect students and companies. Students can acquaint themselves with companies for potential internships, thesis projects, or career opportunities. This is achieved through company visits, where the group spends a portion of a day at each participating company for an in-house experience.
