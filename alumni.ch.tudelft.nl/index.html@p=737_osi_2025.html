On-Stage Interview - May 9th 2025

April 24, 2025 admin

Tickets for alumni are free and available via wisv.ch/alumniosi.

During this year’s On-Stage Interview, a panel will be interviewed on the topic of AI and healthcare. The panel consists of Nima Salami, Joris Broeren, and Jesse Krijthe.

Nima Salami is founder of OASYS NOW. His start-up has a mission to build a privacy-first way for people to have grip on their health data and be matched with clinical trials. Joris Broeren is anesthesiologist and Chief medical Data Officer at HAGA Ziekenhuis. He will provide his view upon AI in healthcare as a medical specialist. Jesse Krijthe is assistant professor in the Pattern Recognition & Bioinformatics group at the TU, he will provide an academical view.

The interview will take place in the Aula and there will be a borrel afterwards. You are welcome from 19:30, the interview will start at 20:00.
