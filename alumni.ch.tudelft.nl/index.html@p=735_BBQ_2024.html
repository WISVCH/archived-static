Alumni BBQ - 31st of August 2024

April 22, 2024 admin

Dear Alumnus,

Save the date! We are thrilled to invite you to the Annual Alumni Barbeque of Constantijn Huygens on August 31st. This event promises great food, sunshine, and wonderful company—perfect for reconnecting and having a good time.

Before we fire up the grill, there will be an engaging talk about the exciting new developments at TU Delft that you can choose to attend. However, if you're here just for the BBQ, sunshine, and good company, that's perfectly fine too!

Event Details:

Date: August 31st, 2024

Optional talk: 16:30 (topic to be announced)

BBQ: 17:30

Location: will be announced

Registration: Registration will close once we reach the maximum number of participants. So, make sure to secure your spot early!

We can’t wait to see you there!

(Please note: The alumni BBQ coincides with a larger program of the year 57 reunion, this is intentional.)
