About CoH

The Mathematics and Computer Science Alumni Committee ‘Constantijn Huygens’ was founded to bring together the mathematicians and computer science graduates and PhD graduates of Delft University of Technology and to bring them into contact with each other and with students. Contact with the faculty is also central in the vision of ‘Constantijn Huygens’.

Constantijn Huygens’ is committed to looking after the interests of alumni and does this by organizing lectures, excursions and drinks for its members.

Are you an alumnus but haven’t signed up yet? Then you can now register online at the TU Delft alumni portal (https://www.tudelftforlife.nl/). Membership is free of charge and thanks to this membership you are invited to all activities of ‘Constantijn Huygens’.
