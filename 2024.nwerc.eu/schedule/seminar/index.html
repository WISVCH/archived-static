Coach seminar

From 10:00 until 11:00 on Sunday in Building 35 - Coaches hall

The coach seminar will give an update on the latest developments and backgrounds on the World Finals 2023 and 2024, the European Championships and the growth of NWERC in the future. These points can be discussed further during the subregional and general coach meetings.

The coach spectator room is on the first floor and can also be used to work in silence.

Location

The Coach seminar takes place in Education Building 35. The coaches hall is located on the second floor.

For directions, see the Location page.
