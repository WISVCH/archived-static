Contact

The organisation can be contacted at contact@2024.nwerc.eu. Companies interested in supporting the contest can contact extern@2024.nwerc.eu.

NWERC Coach Slack

Since a couple of years, there is a NWERC Coaches Slack. Coaches are invited to join here for quick access to the organisation for questions.

Organisation

The NWERC 2024 is hosted by the Delft University of Technology and organised by the CHipCie committee of study association W.I.S.V. ‘Christiaan Huygens’.
